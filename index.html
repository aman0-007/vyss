Who We Are

Vivek Yuv Seva Shakti was founded on the principle that the youth are the architects of the future. We provide a platform for young individuals to engage in meaningful social service, develop leadership skills, and contribute to nation-building. Our name, translating to "Youth Service Power," reflects our core belief in the transformative potential of young people.

Inspired by the ideals of service and selflessness, we channel the energy and enthusiasm of the youth into constructive activities that address pressing social needs.

Our Mission

To empower youth by providing them with opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and social responsibility, thereby creating a better and more prosperous tomorrow for all.
